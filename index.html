Everything you need

One app. Complete farm control.

Built specifically for Nigerian catfish farming — from fingerling stocking to smoked fish sales.

🪣
Pond Management
Add unlimited ponds, track batches, monitor weekly growth, and get harvest projections based on real weight data.
📝
Daily Feed Logs
Log feeding by brand and pellet size. Auto-calculates feed cost per kg. Tracks mortality and water quality daily.
⚖️
Weight Tracker
Record weekly weight samples per pond. View growth curves and FCR trends. Know exactly when to harvest.
🎯
Harvest Records
Record wet or dry harvests per pond. Dry yield auto-calculated (live ÷ 4). Harvest costs auto-logged to expenses.
🐟
Fish Stock Inventory
Auto-tracks live catfish per pond and smoked stock — Whole (Small/Medium/Big/Jumbo) and Cutlets (Head/Tail/Middle/Mixed).
🛒
Sales Tracker
Record live or smoked fish sales. Tracks customers, outstanding payments, and updates inventory automatically.
💰
Expense Manager
All costs auto-logged — fingerlings, feed, harvest labour, fuel, charcoal. Full P&L at a glance.
🌾
Feed Inventory
Track feed brands by pellet size and bag weight. Low-stock alerts. Feed cost auto-deducted when you log daily feeding.
📈
Reports & Analytics
P&L by pond or farm-wide. FCR trends. Revenue vs expenses charts. Harvest history. Everything at your fingertips.
Simple process

Up and running in minutes.

Create Your Account

Add Your Ponds
Enter each pond's size, type and capacity. Stock your fingerlings with a single form — cost auto-logs to expenses.
